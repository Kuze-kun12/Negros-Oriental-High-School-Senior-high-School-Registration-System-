ABOUT OUR WEBSITE

To our website serves as the official online enrollment platform for Negros Oriental High School - Senior High School, streamlining the registration process for students, parents, and school administrators. Designed with user-friendliness in mind, it enables a fast, secure, and efficient enrollment experience, minimizing paperwork and reducing long queues. Using Black Box Testing techniques, we ensure the system’s reliability, accuracy, and performance, making it easier to submit, process, and track enrollment applications. With enhanced security measures, seamless accessibility, and a well-structured interface, our platform provides a hassle-free digital solution for a smooth and convenient enrollment process.

RESEARCH INFORMATION

Our research focuses on enhancing the online enrollment system of Negros Oriental High School - Senior High School through Black Box Testing, ensuring its functionality, reliability, and efficiency. This study addresses common enrollment challenges such as long queues, manual data entry errors, and processing delays by implementing a user-friendly and secure digital platform. By evaluating system performance, we aim to streamline the enrollment process for students, parents, and school administrators while reducing the workload for staff. With an optimized system, school staff can manage student records more efficiently, minimize errors, and improve overall administrative operations, leading to a smoother and more organized enrollment experience for all stakeholders.
