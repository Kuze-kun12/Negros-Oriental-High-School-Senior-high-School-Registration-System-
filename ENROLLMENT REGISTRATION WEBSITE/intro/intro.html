ABOUT OUR WEBSITE

TOur website serves as the official online enrollment platform for Negros Oriental High School - Senior High School, streamlining the registration process for students, parents, and school administrators. Designed with user-friendliness in mind, it enables a fast, secure, and efficient enrollment experience, minimizing paperwork and reducing long queues. Using Black Box Testing techniques, we ensure the system’s reliability, accuracy, and performance, making it easier to submit, process, and track enrollment applications. With enhanced security measures, seamless accessibility, and a well-structured interface, our platform provides a hassle-free digital solution for a smooth and convenient enrollment process.

RESEARCH INFORMATION

Our research focuses on enhancing the online enrollment system of Negros Oriental High School - Senior High School through Black Box Testing. This method evaluates the system’s functionality, reliability, and efficiency by testing inputs and expected outputs without examining the internal code. The study aims to address common enrollment challenges, such as long queues, manual data entry errors, and inefficiencies, by implementing a user-friendly, secure, and streamlined digital platform. By identifying system flaws and improving performance, this research contributes to a more effective enrollment process for students, parents, and school administrators, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free experience.
